A wonderful journey of civilization

Book rafting originated in Europe in the 1960s and 1970s, readers read their own books, casually placed in public places, such as park benches, the person who picked up the book can walk away to read, read it and then put it back into public places, and then drifted it out, let the next book lover read, continue a period of drifting books.

Knowledge is beautiful because of the spread

There is no proof of borrowing, no deposit, no loan term. This kind of good book sharing way, let "knowledge is beautiful because of the spread". Nowadays, the way of book rafting is not limited to the outdoor one, more and more imaginative book friends in the drifting book, in the drift description set their own drift rules, so that the book rafting process becomes more colorful.
